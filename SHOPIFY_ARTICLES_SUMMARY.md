## Article 2: Israeli Payment Gateways Comparison

### Metadata
- **ID:** `shopify-payment-gateways-israel`
- **Title:** שערי תשלום לחנות Shopify בישראל: השוואה מלאה
- **Slug:** `shopify-payment-gateways-israel`
- **Category:** E-commerce
- **Read Time:** 11 minutes
- **Date:** 20 ינואר 2025
- **Word Count:** ~1,200 words
- **Image:** https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556742202-5c1fc3b0eb5f?w=800&q=80&fm=webp&fit=crop

### Content Structure

#### What is a Payment Gateway
- Definition and purpose
- Security verification
- Money transfer process
- Fee structure (percentage + fixed fee)

#### 1. Paybox - The King in Israel

**Fees:**
- Credit card: 2.37% + 25 אגורה
- Debit card: 1.5% + 25 אגורה
- Bit: 2.37% + 25 אגורה
- Klarna: 2.37% + 25 אגורה

**Advantages:**
- Perfect Shopify integration
- Well-known in Israel
- Hebrew support
- All Israeli payment methods
- Quick settlement (usually next day for 500₪+)

**Disadvantages:**
- Higher fees compared to some alternatives
- Customer support can be slow
- Competitive market pressure

**Cost Example:** 100,000₪ monthly sales = ~2,370₪ in fees

#### 2. PayPal

**Fees:**
- 2.9% + 30 cents per transaction (USD)

**Advantages:**
- International payment method
- Globally recognized
- Customers with foreign accounts prefer it

**Disadvantages:**
- Lower preference in Israel
- Currency conversion costs
- Low demand in Israeli market

**Verdict:** Only for international businesses

#### 3. Stripe

**Fees:**
- Local cards: 2.2% + 50 אגורה
- Foreign cards: 2.9% + 50 אגורה

**Advantages:**
- Lower fees comparatively
- Modern technology
- Beautiful interface
- USD account support
- Great for international businesses

**Disadvantages:**
- Still developing in Israel
- Limited Bit support
- Less known in Israel
- Longer settlement times

**When to choose:** International business or high volume (100,000₪+/month)

#### 4. Other Options

- **2Checkout (Verifone):** 3.5% + 95 cents - Too expensive
- **Authorize.net:** 2.9%+ - Not adapted to Israel

### Detailed Comparison Table

**Scenario:** 50,000₪/month, 333 transactions at 150₪ average

| Gateway | Percentage | Fixed Fee | Cost/Transaction | Monthly Total |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|--------------|
| Paybox | 2.37% | 25 אגורה | 1.31₪ | 1,584₪ |
| Stripe | 2.2% | 50 אגורה | 1.20₪ | 1,450₪ |
| PayPal | 2.9% (USD) | 30¢ | ~1.75₪ | 2,100₪ |

**Savings with Stripe:** 134₪/month = 1,608₪/year
**Loss with PayPal:** 516₪/month = 6,192₪/year

### Decision Factors

1. **Customer Type**
   - Israel only → Paybox
   - Many international → Stripe or PayPal

2. **Product Type**
   - Physical products (low cost) → Paybox
   - Digital/courses → Stripe

3. **Sales Volume**
   - Under 10,000₪/month → Paybox
   - Over 100,000₪/month → Stripe

4. **Support Needs**
   - Hebrew support needed → Paybox
   - Technical team available → Stripe

### Final Recommendations

**For Small/Medium Israeli Stores (up to 100,000₪/month)**
→ Choose Paybox: Simple, trusted, familiar to Israelis

**For International or Advanced Stores**
→ Choose Stripe: Modern technology, lower fees, better for growth

**Do NOT Choose**
→ PayPal (unless expecting lots of international sales with existing PayPal accounts)

### Money Saving Tips
1. Negotiate with competitors
2. Batch transactions for lower per-transaction fees
3. Account for refund fees
4. Online payments are cheaper than checks/cash

### Profit Impact Analysis
40% profit margin example:
- Paybox fees reduce profit by 6%
- Stripe fees reduce profit by 4.6%
- PayPal fees reduce profit by 7%
